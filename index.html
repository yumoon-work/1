Yu Gyeong Moon is a visual designer in Seoul, KR, who loves to play with typography, branding, web, and printed matters. Inspired by Alvar Aalto’s words, “Don’t forget to play,” she sees playfulness as a key part of the creative process and enjoys experimenting to push design boundaries.

Hi there, thanks for stopping by! : ) I recently graduated from Hongik University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Communication Design. To me, designing is like being in love. As a designer, I believe the difference between good and great lies in the details.
