When I listened to the Create Great Podcast, Oskar said this.

“Branding is finding that story. It’s so much about storytelling and finding that strong concept...”

Yes, It’s all about stories. Even this introduction as well...

↑ ← →

So I’ve tried to come up with a brief story about myself. Please don’t blame me, if I’m a little weird.

↑ ← →

I’m Yu Gyeong Moon, a junior designer specializing in typography, branding, and creative coding. I see myself as a creative worker interested in playful, and thoughtful design.

↑ ← →

Sometimes I’m called as a typography enthusiasist, a writer, a programmer, a swimmer, a farmer and so on. But in five years, I hope to introduce myself simply as a designer and a coder.

↑ ← →

I made this small website as a presentation for you. I’ll share the address of this website later on, so you can see this through it freely.

↑ ← →

And of course, all the text is set in ‘bold’. Just like ‘being bold’.

↑ ← →

Anyway, let’s start.

↑ ← →

Where it all started

↑ ← →

Born in Korea, I’ve studied my BFA in Visual Communication Design in Seoul. Back in school, I was a member of the school typography club.

↑ ← →

I’ve also been an avid reader of design books. One day, I came across this..

↑ ← →

Ruben Pater, The Politics of Design, 2016

‘A design cannot be disconnected from the values and assumptions in which it was created, from the ideologies behind it. It can be difficult to see how visual communication and ideology are related because ideology is in everything around us, we perceive it as natural.’

↑ ← →

After reading this, I began to think about how closely design is tied to values, ideologies, culture, and context. And just felt that I needed to get out into the world to experience more design.

↑ ← →

Then I went on an exchange program in the US. There, I met great mentors who worked at The New York Times and Pentagram...

↑ ← →

Later, I did some design internship in both Seoul and Helsinki, while applying or expanding my design practices...

↑ ← →

Professionally, I’m still in my 20s, and I want to explore more and experience things beyond where I come from.

↑ ← →

And now currently looking for my “First real job”.

↑ ← →

As a designer I believe the difference between good and great lies in the details. And trying to stay playful, curious, and positive.

↑ ← →

Is that all?

Brand Identity, 2024.

↑ ← →

Searchlight is a collaborative platform that introduces artists and their works to the public and connects them with audiences and consumers.

↑ ← →

Challenge.

It previously had too expressive identity, that couldn’t stand the time. And this platform aims to illuminate individual artists as active subjects.

↑ ← →

So I thought about building the identity around a strong, minimal typographic system that keeps the focus on featured projects.

↑ ← →

Design Direction.
‘Unveil the Rising Artists’

Clear concept inspired by the name itself. Using a light motif to discover and support the hidden talent.
