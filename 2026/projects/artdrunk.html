Client. artdrunk

Year. 2024

The Seoul Art Guide is a book published by ArtDrunk, a global media brand that explores the world through art for everyone. The book introduces exciting exhibitions to see in Seoul in autumn 2024, curated through the eyes of ArtDrunk. It also features a road trip outside of Seoul to explore the diverse Korean art scene, the Gwangju Biennale, and a wealth of art world news, including food and cocktail recommendations from the artists themselves. We used a dark blue cover and autumnal colours for the guidebook to capture the sentiment of travellers heading out to see exhibitions during the autumn season. We also designed the book to be lightweight and easy to carry when travelling, using thick, lightweight paper.
