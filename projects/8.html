Searchlight is a collaborative platform formed by designers and artists dedicated to shedding light on social issues through cultural projects. Everyday Practice designed and developed both the brand identity and website to reflect this collective vision. The identity is built from a strong typographic system and minimal graphic elements that keeps the focus on featured projects.
