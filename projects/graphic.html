Yu Gyeong Moon is a designer in between Seoul and Helsinki, who loves to play with typography, branding, web, and printed matters. Inspired by Alvar Aalto’s words, “Don’t forget to play,” she sees playfulness as a key part of the creative process and enjoys experimenting to push design boundaries.

Hi there, thanks for stopping by : ) I studied Visual Communication Design at Hongik University in Seoul, KR, graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts. To me, designing is like being in love. The process of design—from transforming challenges into visual insights and solving problems creatively—has always fascinated me. As a designer, I believe the difference between good and great lies in the details.
