Yu Gyeong Moon is a designer in between Seoul and Helsinki, who loves to play with typography, branding, web, and printed matters. Inspired by Alvar Aalto’s words, “Don’t forget to play,” she sees playfulness as a key part of the creative process and enjoys experimenting to push design boundaries.
