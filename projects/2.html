#
Infographic
Title
Hop on!
Type
Student work at MICA
Year
2023

Hop on! is an infographic project that illuminates one aspect of my life. Since I am a bike enthusiast and have data sources to mine by using public bicycles, I dive into this theme. Data from June to August 2023 were researched. The key visual was motivated by bike wheels, road graphics, and color schemes. It tells about my riding histories in 3 categories: Frequency, Distance, and Road Condition.
* Informative texts for cyclists were from HSL.
