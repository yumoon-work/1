2024. 08.
As part of the Everyday Practice team, we designed the Seoul Art Guidebook, published by ArtDrunk, a global media brand that explores the world through art for everyone. The Seoul Art Guidebook introduces exciting exhibitions to see in Seoul in autumn 2024, curated through the eyes of ArtDrunk. We designed the book to be lightweight and easy to carry when travelling, using thick, lightweight paper.
