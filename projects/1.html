#
Graphic, Poster Series
Title
Play with T
Purpose
Student Work at Hongik Univ.
Year
2022

Play with T is a series of graphic posters playing with time management, texture, and typography. The main goal of this series was to exercise graphic languages. So I focused on ‘how’ to express in visual languages from typography to photography, and from drawing to overprinting. While working on it, I was reminded of one of the quotes I like; “Don’t forget to play!”
